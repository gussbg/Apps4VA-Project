About The Source Awakens

In this project, The Source Awakens intends to show the trends in SOL scores and density of different ethnic minorities. For example, if a school has a higher percentage of African-American students, do these students perform better than schools with a lower percentage of African-American students? What are the average test scores of Asian students where a schoolâ€™s student body is composed of more than 50% Asian students? These are a few of the questions this project attempts to answer.

In this project, there are many charts that help to answer these questions. The first chart shows the trends across years in SOL scores for a particular race as selected by the user. It compares these trends to the trends across years in the average score for a chosen school. The second chart shows the race composition of the school as a pie chart. As the race composition changes from year to year, the user can select which school year to see the composition for.
